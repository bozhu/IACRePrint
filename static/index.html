IACR ePrint Updates

This is an unofficial twitter feed for
the IACR Cryptology ePrint Archive.

Our bot is monitoring IACR's website, and generating tweets for all new, revised and withdrawn papers.

Tweets are formatted as "[update_type] title (authors) iacr_link". If tweets are too long (over the 140 char limit), the parts before iacr_link will be truncated.
